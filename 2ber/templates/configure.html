Controllers can be relay boards (STR116, STR008), or a PID (Omega CN7500). Later, you can add a device to each controller. You can add relays to relay boards, or thermostats to PIDs. You'll need an address for each device.

Relays can control a few different types of valves, and pumps.

Thermostats will give you a temperature reading, and let you set a target temperature.
